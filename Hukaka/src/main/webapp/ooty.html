Welcome to Ooty!

Ooty, also known as Udagamandalam, is a popular hill station in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India. It is famous for its pleasant climate, picturesque landscapes, and tea gardens. Ooty is a favored destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and honeymooners alike.

Surrounded by lush greenery, Ooty offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Its attractions include botanical gardens, lakes, waterfalls, and colonial-era architecture. The toy train journey from Mettupalayam to Ooty is a must-do experience, offering breathtaking views of the Nilgiri Mountains.

  • Doddabetta Peak

    • Location: Doddabetta Road, Ooty
    • Timings: 9:00 am to 6:00 pm; every day
    • Entry fee: INR 10 per person
    • Attractions: Doddabetta Peak is the highest point in the Nilgiri Mountains and offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
    • Facilities: Visitors can enjoy trekking trails, a telescope house, and a small snack bar at the peak.
    • Contact: For more information, contact +91-XXXXXXXXXX.

    • Location: North Lake Road, Ooty
    • Timings: 9:00 am to 6:00 pm; every day
    • Entry fee: Boating charges apply (varies based on the type of boat)
    • Attractions: Ooty Lake offers boating facilities and is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a popular picnic spot.
    • Facilities: Visitors can rent pedal boats, rowboats, and motorboats for a leisurely ride on the lake.

  • Government Rose Garden

    • Location: Horticulture House, Elk Hill Road, Ooty
    • Timings: 8:00 am to 6:00 pm; every day
    • Entry fee: INR 30 per person
    • Attractions: Government Rose Garden is one of the largest rose gardens in India, featuring over 20,000 varieties of roses.
