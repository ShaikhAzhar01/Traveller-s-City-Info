Welcome to Mumbai!

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra and one of the most populous cities in India. Located on the western coast of India, Mumbai is the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of the country. It is a bustling metropolis with a vibrant mix of cultures, languages, and traditions..

Mumbai is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel. The city is also home to Bollywood, the Hindi film industry, which produces the largest number of films in the world. Its vibrant nightlife, diverse cuisine, and shopping districts make it a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.
