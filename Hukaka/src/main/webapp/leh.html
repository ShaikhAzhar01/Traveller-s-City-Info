Welcome to Leh!

Leh, located in the northern Indian state of Ladakh, is a mesmerizing destination known for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and adventurous activities. Situated at an altitude of about 3,500 meters above sea level, Leh offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and is a haven for nature enthusiasts and thrill-seekers.

With its rich cultural heritage and spiritual ambiance, Leh attracts travelers from around the world seeking solace and adventure amidst the majestic mountains. The town is dotted with monasteries, stupas, and palaces, each narrating a tale of Leh's vibrant history and traditions.
